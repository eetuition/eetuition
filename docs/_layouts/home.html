Our Values and Ethos

EETuition does understand that each child is unique and uniquely talented. Hence, each child’s need is different and should be given opportunity to reach their full potential.

EETuition works for every child because at first we analyse the strengths and the weaknesses of each child, design a tailored study plan and provide the appropriate support that he/she needs.

EETuition expertly designs a bespoke study plan having the perfect blend of books and worksheets personalised for your child, so that your child gets exactly what he/she needs. We do encourage parents to be involved in the whole learning journey, so that they are well informed.

EETuition works with each child to make them feel motivated and confident. We also give parents the opportunity to see the improvement in their child’s performance and their confidence level.

EETuition encourages Meaningful Learning over just ‘learning’ which makes EETuition stands out in the crowd of numerous tuition providers and makes children feel enthusiastic and enjoy the whole learning journey.

EETuition uses CPA (Concrete-Pictorial-Abstract) approach effectively and efficiently that enhances children’s learning.
